Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my flight is delayed? expand_more
No worries! We monitor flight statuses. If you've provided your flight number, we'll know. For significant delays extending beyond your booking period, standard hourly rates may apply upon exit.
Is the shuttle service running 24/7? expand_more
Yes, our VIP shuttles run continuously 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. During peak hours (5 AM - 11 PM), shuttles depart every 10-15 minutes. Overnight, they run on-demand or every 20 minutes.
Can I cancel my reservation for a full refund? expand_more
Absolutely. Cancellations made at least 24 hours before your scheduled arrival time are eligible for a 100% refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a small service fee.
